SOULMATECHINE

SOULMATECHINE located in China (Hangzhou) Computing Power Town, is an innovative enterprise dedicated to the in-house development of domestic GPT language large models and image-text large models. The company leverages an industrialization platform jointly established by the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) and the Linping District government, bringing together core algorithm experts from prestigious overseas universities and renowned domestic tech companies to form a high-level team of postgraduate and doctoral researchers.
